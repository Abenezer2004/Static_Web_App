All Might

Basic Information

Real Name: Toshinori Yagi

Hero Name: All Might

Age: 40s

Height: 220cm

Quirk: One For All

School: U.A. High School

Role: No.1 Pro Hero

Backstory

All Might, whose real name is Toshinori Yagi, is the former No. 1 Hero and the Symbol of Peace in the world of My Hero Academia. He was born with the Quirk "One For All," which allows him to stockpile and transfer power to others. All Might inherited this Quirk from his predecessor and became a symbol of hope and justice for society.

Throughout his career, All Might fought against villains and protected the innocent, earning him immense respect and admiration. However, due to a severe injury sustained in a battle with his arch-nemesis All For One, All Might's body was severely damaged, limiting his ability to use his powers effectively. Despite this, he continued to serve as a mentor and symbol of hope for aspiring heroes, particularly for Midoriya Izuku (Deku), whom he chose as his successor to inherit One For All.

Abilities

One For All: All Might's primary ability is One For All, which allows him to stockpile and transfer power. This Quirk grants him superhuman strength, speed, agility, and durability. However, due to his injury, he can only use a fraction of his full power.

Combat Skills: All Might is an incredibly skilled hand-to-hand combatant, utilizing his immense strength and speed to overpower opponents. He has developed various fighting techniques and strategies over his career as a hero.

First Appearance and Personality

All Might first appears in Chapter 1 of the My Hero Academia manga and Episode 1 of the anime adaptation.
