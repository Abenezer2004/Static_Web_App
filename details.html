Backstory

All Might's journey began as the humble disciple of Nana Shimura, inheriting the power of One For All. After her tragic death, he carried both immense power and guilt, vowing to become the world's unwavering Symbol of Peace. For decades, his smiling persona single-handedly lowered global crime rates and embodied hope. However, a brutal fight with his arch-nemesis, All For One, left him with a crippling injury that steadily drained his power and time. He now races against his own fading light to find a worthy successor, a quest that leads him to a determined, quirkless boy named Izuku Midoriya.

Abilities

One For All: All Might's primary ability is One For All, which allows him to stockpile and transfer power. This Quirk grants him superhuman strength, speed, agility, and durability. However, due to his injury, he can only use a fraction of his full power.

Combat Skills: All Might is an incredibly skilled hand-to-hand combatant, utilizing his immense strength and speed to overpower opponents. He has developed various fighting techniques and strategies over his career as a hero.

First Appearance and Personality

All Might first appears in Chapter 1 of the My Hero Academia manga and Episode 1 of the anime adaptation.
